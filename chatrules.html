These rules apply to all yoteachapp chatrooms for Dr. Pang's courses and other academic activities. These rules may be updated from time to time.

Please be aware that all chatrooms are public. Even people outside the class can read the messages. Messages may or may not be deleted at the end of semesters.

A message may be removed if:
i) It is a test message;
ii) It is spam or advertising;
iii) It repeats the content of another message;
iv) It contains personal information (e.g. phone numbers or emails);
v) It is hurtful to other students, instructors, anyone in the HKBU community or beyond;
vi) It is suspected to be from someone not in the class for which the chatroom is designed;
vii) It is not from the current semester.
If a message of the above types is constructive (e.g. ii - advertising an academic event; iv - giving out your own email to make study groups; vi - asking if you should take the course) then it may be allowed in the chatroom for a short period of time before being deleted.

Instructors may disallow a student from posting if he/she posts too many messages of the above types.
