Some information on the exams

Midterm 1 will cover the geometry (Sections 10.2, 10.4, 10.5, 10.1, 12.1) and single and double integration (Sections 5.1-5.5, 14.1, 14.2, 14.4 - polar coordinates part only, NOT the general change of coordinates), but NOT integration by parts (Section 6.1) and NOT improper integrals (Sections 6.5, 14.3). This is everything in the slides labelled Weeks 1-5 EXCEPT Week 5 p20, and all the exercise sheet questions from the beginning until sheet #10.

Midterm 2 will focus on triple integration (Sections 14.5, 14.6), improper integrals (Sections 6.5, 14.3), integration by parts (Section 6.1) and multivariable differentiation (Sections 12.2-12.7, 12.9). This is everything in the slides labelled Weeks 6-10 EXCEPT Week 10 p23, and all the exercise sheet questions from sheet #11 until and including sheet #18. The questions can also require material already tested on Midterm 1.

  • The midterms and the final are closed book exams. You will be given the formulae sheet.
  • Calculators are allowed, for arithmetic only.
  • The questions on the midterms and final will require you to show all your work, and there will be partial credit available.
  • All homework questions are fair game for the midterm and final. However, there will also be questions unlike those on the homework, which will require you to think on your feet. All questions will be solvable using material from class and from homework.
