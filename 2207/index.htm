Some email etiquette:
  • Please put "Math 2207" in the subject, and what you are asking about e.g. "Math 2207: ???". If you are emailing after the semester has ended, please also put "Fall 2016" in the subject.
  • If emailing the instructor, please allow 48 hours for a response. Do NOT send a follow-up email or ask in person before 48 hours. A response from TAs may require more than 48 hours.
  • If you are emailing about administrative issues, please use your @hkbu address, and include your student ID.
  • To include equations etc. in your emails, please attach a .pdf or .jpeg. (Take a screenshot or photo or scan, or use a PDF printer.) No MS word documents or raw latex files please.
  • The dates in gray below are tentative and subject to change.
  • Homework is due at ?? on the due date. No extensions will be granted under any circumstances.
  • You are encourged to work with your classmates, but you must write up your solution by yourself - this is to ensure that you understand the solution, and can solve similar problems on the exams.
